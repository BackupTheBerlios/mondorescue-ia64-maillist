Humm If it's not bootable, how did you make  a mondorestore from it ?
Also mondorestore is looking for specific files on the CD, that are not
available on a mindi CD.

So I really advise you to use the CD produced by mondoarchive instead.
Another warning try to use coherent versions of mondo (2.2.1) and mindi
(1.2.1). You'll also need mindi-busybox (1.2.2)

However, be warned I've not tested ia64 support recently, and especially
not since I added mindi-busybox. So it may create a problem.
